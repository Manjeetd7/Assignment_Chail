5 Best Hotels to Stay in Chail

Sterling Shivalik Chail

Set in Chail, 32 km from Victory Tunnel, Sterling Shivalik Chail offers accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking and a restaurant. This 4-star resort offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The accommodation features airport transfers, while a car rental service is also available. At the resort, all rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries, some units at Sterling Shivalik Chail also have a city view. All guest rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle.

Rosewood Estate

Located 37 km from Victory Tunnel, Rosewood Estate provides accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking. Some of the units include a satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave, and a private bathroom with a bath and bathrobes. Guests at the bed and breakfast can enjoy a vegetarian breakfast. Rosewood Estate offers a picnic area. A an indoor play area is also available at the accommodation, while guests can also relax on the sun terrace. Circular Road is 35 km from Rosewood Estate, while Jakhoo Gondola is 35 km away. The nearest airport is Simla Airport, 57 km from the bed and breakfast.

Welcomehotel by ITC Hotels

Situated in Chail, 38 km from Victory Tunnel, Welcomhotel by ITC Hotels, Tavleen, Chail features accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a garden. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi. The accommodation provides a concierge service, and currency exchange for guests. The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. All rooms at Welcomhotel by ITC Hotels, Tavleen, Chail have air conditioning and a desk. The accommodation offers 5-star accommodation with a sauna and terrace.

Treehouse Chail Villas

Situated in Chail, 37 km from Victory Tunnel, Treehouse Chail Villas features accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. The property is located 35 km from Circular Road, 35 km from Jakhoo Gondola and 35 km from Jakhu Temple. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, room service and free WiFi. At the resort, rooms are equipped with a wardrobe. Rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Treehouse Chail Villas, rooms come with a TV and a safety deposit box. Continental and vegetarian breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation.

Chails Hamlet

Situated in Chail, 37 km from Victory Tunnel, Chails Hamlet features accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a restaurant. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. Guests can have a drink at the bar. All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a safety deposit box. Rooms are equipped with a kettle and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, while some rooms have a kitchen fitted with a fridge. At Chails Hamlet every room is fitted with bed linen and towels.
